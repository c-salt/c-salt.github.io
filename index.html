Game Rules:

The goal of isolation is to block your opponent in.

Start by placing your first piece anywhere on the board. After that you can move like a chess queen (vertically, horizontally, diagonally) as long as your path is completely open (no walls, or other players in your way). The game is over when a player does not have any available moves.
